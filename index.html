00 — About

Leone Morandini — Torino, 2024

Born in the mountains,
shaped by human interaction.

I'm Leone, born in 2001 in Piedmont, Italy. I studied Transportation Design at IAAD Turin and I'm currently completing a Master in Digital Communication Design. My work moves between visual storytelling, content creation, photography and cultural communication. Years spent as an educator, photographer and event organizer shaped the way I design: by listening first.

I believe the best design is the kind you don't notice. The kind that just feels right, that talks to the right people in the right language.
