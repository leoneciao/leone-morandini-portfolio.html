I'm Leone, born 2001 in Piedmont, Italy. I studied Transportation Design at IAAD Turin and I'm currently finishing a Master in Digital Communication Design. I've spent years working with people — as an educator, a photographer, an event organizer — and that's exactly where my design thinking comes from.

I believe the best design is the kind you don't notice. The kind that just feels right, that talks to the right people in the right language.
